Tips for Best Results

Follow these guidelines to get the cleanest cut-outs.

  • ✓ Use high contrast photos. Images where the subject clearly stands out from the background (e.g., a person against a plain wall) produce the cleanest results.
  • ✓ Good lighting matters. Well-lit subjects with defined edges are much easier for AI to detect and separate accurately.
  • ✓ Avoid motion blur. Sharp, in-focus images always produce better cut-outs than blurry or low-resolution photos.
  • ✓ Download as PNG. Always save your background-removed image as a PNG file to preserve the transparent background. Saving as JPG will add a white background.
  • ✓ Resize after removing the background. For social media use, remove the background first, then use our Image Resizer to get the exact dimensions you need.
