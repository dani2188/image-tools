Privacy Policy

At QuickImageTools, we respect your privacy.

1. Image Processing

All image processing (resize, crop, etc.) happens directly in your browser. We do not upload or store your images on our servers.

2. Background Removal Tool

The background remover tool uses third-party APIs to process images. Uploaded images may be temporarily sent to these services for processing.

3. Cookies and Ads

We may use third-party services like Google AdSense to display ads. These services may use cookies to personalize content.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
