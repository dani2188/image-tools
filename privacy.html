Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Introduction

QuickImageTools ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at quickimagetools.onrender.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using our website, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Image Processing

Image Resizer: All image resizing is performed locally in your web browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded to our servers or transmitted over the internet. We have no access to the images you resize, and no image data is stored by us in any form.

Background Remover: The background removal feature sends your uploaded image to a third-party AI service (remove.bg) to perform the processing. Your image is transmitted securely (over HTTPS) and is used solely to produce the background-removed result. According to remove.bg's privacy policy, uploaded images are not stored permanently or used for any purpose other than processing your request.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from visitors to QuickImageTools. We do not operate user accounts, email lists, or any registration system.

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as page views and general geographic region) through standard web analytics. This data cannot be used to identify any individual user.

Google's advertising cookies allow Google and its partners to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data from sites that use its services, see: How Google uses data from sites that use our services.
